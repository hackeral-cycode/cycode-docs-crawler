Software Composition Analysis (SCA) policies detect potential issues with vulnerabilities and licenses with open source dependencies used in your organization.

The **SCA scanner** examines open-source components and their dependencies for known vulnerabilities to identify and mitigate risks associated with third-party libraries, and ensure that they don’t introduce security vulnerabilities into your codebase.
