**Last updated**: January 4, 2026

To support the delivery of our SaaS services, Cycode engages and uses a number of data processors ("**sub-processors**").

A sub-processor is an external service or third-party provider that is engaged by Cycode to deliver our service to you when we operate as a data processer, that may process certain personal information of Cycode customers’ end users for specific purposes. The type of data that sub-processors have access to is limited to only what is reasonably required to perform the service provided.

Each sub-processor enters into a written contract with Cycode that enforces compliance with applicable data protection laws. Cross-border transfers are conducted under applicable approved mechanisms. This page provides an up-to-date list of the identity, locations and purpose of engagement of sub-processors by Cycode.
