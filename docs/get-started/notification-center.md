## Overview [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

The notification center shows system notifications, so that regular members and admins can stay informed about important updates, errors, and events.

To view your notifications, click the bell icon in the top navigation bar.

## Notification details [Copied!](#notification-details "Copy link to this section")

Notifications are organized in three tabs.

Tab

Description

Important

Alerts that affect your security posture, such as scanning health and new policies.

Mentions

Individual updates that require your attention, such as violation assignments and invites.

Info

General product updates, such as setting changes and system notices.

Additionally, critical issues that can affect core functionality, such as a disconnected integration or broker issue trigger a prominent alert banner upon sign-in. This ensures that the most urgent information is seen and addressed immediately.

## Data Retention [Copied!](#data-retention "Copy link to this section")

Notifications remain in the notification center for 30 days. Afterward, they are removed from the notification center but retained in the database for reference.

Note

If you dismiss a notification, it is no longer visible in the notification center.
