# Managing personal settings using My Profile

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/get-started/account-and-personal-settings/managing-personal-settings-using-my-profile.md "Edit this page")

The My Profile section is where you’ll be able to manage your personal details, change your password, enable multi-factor authentication (MFA), and manage your personal access tokens.

## Accessing your personal settings [Copied!](#accessing-your-personal-settings "Copy link to this section")

To access your personal settings, click the profile icon in the right-hand corner, select **My Profile**, and then select **My Profile** in the left pane.

![](/assets/image-1731589426763.png)

## Profile details [Copied!](#profile-details "Copy link to this section")

In the **Profile Details** section, you’ll see the name and email address used in your Cycode account. You can use this area to change the name in your Cycode account. ![image.png](/assets/image-28354-29.png)

## Changing your password and enabling MFA [Copied!](#changing-your-password-and-enabling-mfa "Copy link to this section")

Use the Security section to change your password and enable multi-factor authentication (MFA).

![](/assets/e2af875-image.png)

### Changing your password [Copied!](#changing-your-password "Copy link to this section")

1.  In the **Change Password** section, click **Change Password**. This displays the Change Password dialog.

![](/assets/5f99631-image.png)

2.  Enter your current password, enter the new password, and then confirm the new password.
    
3.  Click **Save** to save the new password.
    

### Enabling multi-factor authentication (MFA) [Copied!](#enabling-multi-factor-authentication-mfa "Copy link to this section")

Enable multi-factor authentication for an added level of security.

Prerequisite

Make sure you have an authenticator app such as Google Authenticator installed on your phone.

**Note - Currently, Cycode do not enforce users to enable MFA to login to the Cycode system**

To enable MFA:

1.  Toggle the button to enable MFA.
    
2.  Scan the code using the authenticator app installed on your phone. A 6-digit code appears on the screen of your phone. Additionally, a window pops up from Cycode where you can enter those 6 digits.
    
3.  From Cycode, enter the 6-digit code.
    
4.  Save your recovery code in a safe place. The recovery code enables you to log in if you lose your phone.
    

## Generating a Risk Overview report [Copied!](#generating-a-risk-overview-report "Copy link to this section")

You can also enable and generate email reports on your risk overview and have them sent to your email on a scheduled basis.

Risk Overview report is being replaced by Cycode's Reports feature

The email report on your risk overview will be deprecated soon from the My Profile settings, but you’ll still be able to view generate a risk report and much more through our main [Reports feature.](/additional-cycode-features/service-level-agreement-sla/sla-reports)
