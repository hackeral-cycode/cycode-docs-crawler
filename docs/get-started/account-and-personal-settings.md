## Available functionality [Copied!](#available-functionality "Copy link to this section")

Your account and personal settings are accessible from the dropdown located under your avatar in the Cycode platform.

Hover over your avatar to see the following settings:

Setting

Description

Viewing mode

Select light or dark mode for viewing the platform.

My profile

For information on how to manage your profile settings, go to [Manage personal settings using My Profile](/get-started/account-and-personal-settings/managing-personal-settings-using-my-profile) .

Personal access tokens

For information on how to manage personal access tokens, go to [Manage personal access tokens](/get-started/account-and-personal-settings/managing-personal-access-tokens) .
