## Overview [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

The Cycode agentic platform connects external AI agents and assistants to Cycode’s security context. It provides authenticated, standards-based surfaces that let compatible AI clients query findings, inventory, compliance posture, and audit data — and act on them on your behalf — without leaving the tools your team already uses.
