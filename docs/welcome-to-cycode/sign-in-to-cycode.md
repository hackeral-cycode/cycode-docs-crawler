# Sign in to Cycode

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/welcome-to-cycode/sign-in-to-cycode.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Jan 25, 2026 • Published on Apr 23, 2025

* * *

When your admin invites you to Cycode, you receive an email with your organization’s details and a link for you to sign in.

**To sign in to Cycode**

1.  Go to [app.cycode.com](https://app.cycode.com)
    
2.  In the sign-in page, type your email and click **Continue**.
    

Note

If SSO is configured for your organization, you can sign in using your GitHub or Google account. Click the relevant button to authenticate.

3.  Type your password and click **Submit**.
    
4.  If you’ve already configured multi-factor authentication (MFA), enter your code and click **Login**.
    

Important

Cycode recommends configuring MFA, and requiring MFA for all of your users.

The default view on signing in is the Visibility dashboard.
