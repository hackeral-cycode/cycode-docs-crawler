# Reset password

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/welcome-to-cycode/reset-password.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Jan 25, 2026 • Published on Apr 23, 2025

* * *

Note

If you sign in using SSO, you don’t need a password.

You can change or reset your Cycode password, using one of the methods below:

**To reset your password from the login portal**

1.  Go to [app.cycode.com](https://app.cycode.com/) .
    
2.  In the sign-in page, type your email address and click **Continue**.
    
3.  Click **Forgot Password?** and in the Reset Your Password page, click **Reset Password**.
    
4.  When you receive the reset password email from Cycode, open it and click **Change Password**.
    
5.  In the Change Password page, type your new password and click **Change Password**.
    

You should now be able to sign in with your new password.
