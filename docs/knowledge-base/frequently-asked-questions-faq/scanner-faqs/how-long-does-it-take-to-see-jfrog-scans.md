# How long does it take to see JFrog scans?

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/knowledge-base/frequently-asked-questions-faq/scanner-faqs/how-long-does-it-take-to-see-jfrog-scans.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Nov 25, 2024 • Published on Sep 15, 2024

* * *

## Answer [Copied!](#answer "Copy link to this section")

The scan itself is periodic, and performed once in a day/week. If the scan is monitored, it will be in the scope of the sync, which is once every 20 minutes in JFrog.
