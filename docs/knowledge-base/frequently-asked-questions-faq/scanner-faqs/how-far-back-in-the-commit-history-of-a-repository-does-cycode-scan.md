# How far back in the commit history of a repository does Cycode scan?

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/knowledge-base/frequently-asked-questions-faq/scanner-faqs/how-far-back-in-the-commit-history-of-a-repository-does-cycode-scan.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Nov 25, 2024 • Published on Nov 25, 2024

* * *

## Answer [Copied!](#answer "Copy link to this section")

Cycode will generally scan the commit history of a repository from January 1, 2018, and later.

If you wish to scan the entire history of a specific repository, you can use the [on-demand scan](/scanners/manage-the-scanners/create-an-on-demand-scan) (in the UI) or use the [CLI](/developer-experience/developers/cycode-cli) with a specific parameter.
