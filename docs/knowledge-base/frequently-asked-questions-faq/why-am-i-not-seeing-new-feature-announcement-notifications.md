## Answer [Copied!](#answer "Copy link to this section")

Cycode uses a tool called Beamer to let you know about new features, announcements, important updates, and more.

To ensure that you’re getting a popup notification when something is announced, you’ll need to opt-in to notifications (don’t worry - we promise not to bombard you!). If you’re not seeing the notifications, follow the [instructions in this article](https://www.getbeamer.com/help/how-to-unblock-notification-permissions-on-any-browser) from Beamer to enable them.

And, of course, all the notifications will continue to appear under product updates (see below) and in What’s new at Cycode.
