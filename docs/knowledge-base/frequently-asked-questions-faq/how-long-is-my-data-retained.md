# How long is my data retained?

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/knowledge-base/frequently-asked-questions-faq/how-long-is-my-data-retained.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Dec 3, 2024 • Published on Dec 3, 2024

* * *

Cycode retains violation data and Discovery page data (such as entitles from your technologies and edges) for at least a year.
