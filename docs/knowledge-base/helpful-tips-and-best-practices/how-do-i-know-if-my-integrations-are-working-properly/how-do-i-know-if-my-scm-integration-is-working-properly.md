# How do I know if my SCM integration is working properly?

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/knowledge-base/helpful-tips-and-best-practices/how-do-i-know-if-my-integrations-are-working-properly/how-do-i-know-if-my-scm-integration-is-working-properly.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Nov 19, 2024 • Published on Jun 23, 2024

* * *

Once you’ve completed the integration of your SCM with Cycode’s platform, you can verify that the integration is working properly using the On-Demand Scans.

1.  Go to the Cycode Platform, and select **Settings > Backoffice > On-Demand Scans**.

![](/assets/image-562.png)

2.  Filter the results by **Is Initial Scan = Yes** and then filter for the organization you just integrated.
3.  You can also filter by category, for example **Secrets**.
4.  Verify that scan began, and that there are no cloning issues, and the data shown is the information you filtered for.
