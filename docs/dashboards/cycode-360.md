## Overview [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

Cycode’s dashboards provide insights into your complete AppSec solution. The default view is the Visibility dashboard, where you can view your overall security posture. Guidance on critical security issues and what has been addressed is displayed in the Prioritization and Remediation dashboards.

General Note

Trends and calculations are based on risk scores that have been tracked starting from November 20, 2024. Data from before this date aren’t included in the trends, which may affect the range of information displayed.

To view a broader historical perspective, you can switch to **severity mode**, which provides insights over a longer time range.

Note

By default the dashboards display all projects. You can filter according to a specific project as needed.

### Visibility dashboard [Copied!](#visibility-dashboard "Copy link to this section")

The Visibility dashboard is your central hub for understanding the current state of your organization’s security landscape. The dashboard provides detailed insights into key areas such as integrations, scanner health, and your organization’s general risk score. Additionally, the Visibility dashboard shows a comprehensive overview of the integrated technologies and a snapshot of your risk backlog, so you can maintain full awareness of your security posture at all times.

[Visibility Dashboard Detailed Documentation](/dashboards/cycode-360/visibility-dashboard)

### Prioritization dashboard [Copied!](#prioritization-dashboard "Copy link to this section")

Use the Prioritization dashboard to identify and focus on the most critical security issues within your organization. This dashboard helps you triage and address risks effectively, with a clear view of violations prioritized by risk level, scanner categories, and age. The Prioritization dashboard shows the **top 5 risks**, **Critical and high risk over time**, and a **Prioritization funnel** so you can allocate resources where they matter most and take action to strengthen your security posture.

[Prioritization Dashboard Detailed Documentation](/dashboards/cycode-360/prioritization-dashboard)

### Remediation dashboard [Copied!](#remediation-dashboard "Copy link to this section")

The Remediation dashboard provides a comprehensive view of your organization’s progress in addressing security issues. The dashboard highlights key metrics such as **Detected vs. remediated** violations, performance trend over time, and **SLA status**. With insights into MTTR by risk level and your overall compliance posture, this dashboard helps you track remediation efforts, measure effectiveness, and ensure your organization is on track to meet its security goals.

[Remediation Dashboard Detailed Documentation](/dashboards/cycode-360/remediation-dashboard)
