## Overview [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

The AI toolchain is expanding fast, as developers are adopting AI coding assistants, connecting MCP servers, pulling in AI models and packages, spinning up AI infrastructure, and embedding API keys for AI services. A holistic view of an organization’s ADLC security posture is essential for effective risk management and strategic decision-making. To serve this need, Cycode analyzes the signals that AI tools leave behind in your SDLC and exposes this data in dedicated dashboards.

These ADLC security dashboards provide a centralized, high-level summary of AI-related risks, enabling product managers, security teams, and developers to quickly understand their tenant’s AI exposure and prioritize remediation efforts.

### AI visibility dashboard [Copied!](#ai-visibility-dashboard "Copy link to this section")

The AI visibility dashboard enables users to do the following:

*   View an inventory of all AI infrastructures, models, coding assistants, and MCPs detected in your environment
    
*   Understand which repositories are using AI tools, code assistance, and MCP integrations
    
*   Monitor the spread of AI secrets and AI packages across your codebase
    
*   Monitor developer AI usage patterns including MCPs, interaction scan results, and models
    

### AI risk dashboard [Copied!](#ai-risk-dashboard "Copy link to this section")

The AI risk dashboard enables users to do the following:

*   See an overview of all open AI violations according to risk level and age
    
*   Understand which AI packages carry the highest risk, and which vulnerabilities have the most impact
    
*   Identify the top AI secrets at risk and their distribution by type
    
*   View which projects and repositories have the highest AI-related risk
