# Company custom remediation guidelines

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/policies/company-custom-remediation-guidelines.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Oct 15, 2024 • Published on Oct 15, 2024

* * *

The Company Guideline field is an optional, powerful feature that enables you to add remediation guidelines to both custom and existing Cycode policies. Through this field, you can add steps to remediate violations, provide additional information, and more.

## Adding company custom remediation guidelines to custom policies [Copied!](#adding-company-custom-remediation-guidelines-to-custom-policies "Copy link to this section")

1.  Create a [new custom policy and add instructions to the Company Guideline field](/policies/create-and-manage-custom-policies/) , or in the list of existing policies, click the three-dot option menu and select **Edit**.

![](/assets/f9ec3d2-image.png)

3.  This displays the Edit policy dialog.

![](/assets/4ed3d57-image.png)

3.  Go to the **Company Guideline** field and enter remediation guidelines for this policy. You can also add links to explain the remediation by either pasting a link directly or entering the link in this format: “[link name](the-link.com) ”.
    
4.  Click **Done** to save your changes.
    

The guidelines you add can then be made available not only in the platform, but also in any IDE integrations such as [CLI](/developer-experience/developers/cycode-cli/) , [VS Code Extension](/developer-experience/developers/cycode-ide-plugins/cycode-vs-code-extension-and-compatibles-editors/viewing-and-handling-scan-results) , and the [JetBrains IDE Plugin](/developer-experience/developers/cycode-ide-plugins/cycode-jetbrains-ide-plugin/) .
