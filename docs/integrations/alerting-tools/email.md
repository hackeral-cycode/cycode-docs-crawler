Integrating emails enable Cycode to send information about detected violations in your environments.

After successfully integrating, you can organize and track relevant information in your monitoring service.

## Integrating Email [Copied!](#integrating-email "Copy link to this section")

1.  Go to **Settings > Integrations** and then click **Add Integration**.
    
2.  Under the **Alerting** section, click **Email**.
    

![](/assets/e3c5f6b-image.png)

The following page is displayed:

![](/assets/email-new.png)

3.  Enter a name for your integration.
    
4.  Type in the semi-colon `;` separated list of email recipients who should receive the notification.
    
5.  Select the Cycode policies you want to send alerts about via Email.
    
6.  Click **Done**. The integration is added to the list of Email notifications, where you can add, edit, or delete email integrations.
    

![](/assets/86cea97-image.png)

7.  To add new integrations, click **Add Integration** from the Email integrations area.
