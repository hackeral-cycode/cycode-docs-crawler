# Opsgenie

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/integrations/alerting-tools/opsgenie.md "Edit this page")

* * *

Integrating Opsgenie enables Cycode to send information about detected violations in your environments.

After successfully integrating, you can organize and track relevant information in your monitoring service.

## Permissions [Copied!](#permissions "Copy link to this section")

You’ll need the following permissions to integrate Opsgenie with Cycode.

## Integrating with Opsgenie [Copied!](#integrating-with-opsgenie "Copy link to this section")

1.  Go to **Settings > Integrations** and then click **Add Integration**.
    
2.  Under the Alerting section, click **Opsgenie**.
    

![](/assets/64c6890-image.png)

The following page is displayed:

![](/assets/opsgenie-new.png)

3.  Enter a name for your integration.
    
4.  Configure an API key. See [Opsgenie](https://docs.opsgenie.com/docs/api-integration) for additional details.
    
5.  Enter your Opsgenie API key in Cycode.
    
6.  Select the Cycode policies you want to receive alerts about in Opsgenie.
    
7.  Click **Done**.
    
    Once you click **Done**, the Opsgenie integrations area loads and shows that your integration is connected.
    

![](/assets/opsgenie-integration-confirmation.png)

9.  To add new integrations, click **Add Integration** from the Opsgenie integrations area.
