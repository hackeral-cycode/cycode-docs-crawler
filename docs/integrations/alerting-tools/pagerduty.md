# PagerDuty

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/integrations/alerting-tools/pagerduty.md "Edit this page")

* * *

Integrating PagerDuty enables Cycode to send information about detected violations in your environments.

After successfully integrating, you can organize and track relevant information in your monitoring service.

## Permissions [Copied!](#permissions "Copy link to this section")

## Integrating with Pager Duty [Copied!](#integrating-with-pager-duty "Copy link to this section")

1.  Go to **Settings > Integrations** and then click **Add Integration**.
    
2.  Under the Alerting section, click **Pager Duty**.
    

![](/assets/64c6890-image.png)

The following page is displayed

![](/assets/pagerduty-new.png)

3.  Enter a name for your integration.
    
4.  Configure a service key according to the “Create a Generic Events API Integration“ instructions from the PagerDuty documentation.
    
5.  Enter your PagerDuty service key in Cycode.
    
6.  Select the Cycode policies you want to receive alerts about in PagerDuty.
    
7.  Click **Done**.
    
    Once you click **Done**, the PagerDuty integrations area loads and shows that your integration is connected.
    

![](/assets/pagerduty-integration-confirmation.png)

8.  To add new integrations, click **Add Integration** from the PagerDuty integrations area.
