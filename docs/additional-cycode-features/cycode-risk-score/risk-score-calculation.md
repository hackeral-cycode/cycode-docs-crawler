## Introduction [Copied!](#introduction "Copy link to this section")

Cycode’s Risk Calculation screen (accessible through the platform settings) provides a centralized hub for understanding and monitoring how Cycode determines its complex risk scores. Designed with transparency and ease-of-use in mind, this feature allows your organization to track updates to risk calculations.

The Risk Calculation screen enables you to tailor risk management strategies to meet specific organizational needs.

## Terminology [Copied!](#terminology "Copy link to this section")

Understanding the terms used in Cycode’s Risk Calculation screen is key to making the most of this feature:

*   **Risk Category**: A grouping of violations based on their nature or type, such as SCA, secrets, Dast etc.
    
*   **Risk Factor**: The specific parameters or conditions that influence the risk score within a category. For example, the severity of a vulnerability or its exploitability might serve as a risk factor.
    
*   **Risk Value**: The calculated numerical score assigned to a violation, derived from the values of its contributing risk factors.
    

## Screen layout [Copied!](#screen-layout "Copy link to this section")

![Screenshot 2024-12-03 at 14.20.50.png](/assets/screenshot-202024-12-03-20at-2014-20-50.png)

The Risk Calculation screen includes the following key components:

*   **Visual explanation of the calculation**: A visual representation of how Cycode calculates risk, making it easy to understand the underlying logic and methodology.
    
*   **Violation categories table**: A detailed table that displays:
    
    *   All violation categories.
        
    *   The number of risk factors associated with each category.
        
    *   The last update time of the formula used for calculation.
        
*   **Detailed parameter view**: By clicking on any row in the table, you can access:
    
    *   All the risk parameters related to the specific violation category.
        
    *   A breakdown of how these parameters contribute to the risk score.
        

This layout is designed to provide both a high-level overview and the ability to dive into the specifics, ensuring comprehensive transparency into Cycode’s risk assessment process.
