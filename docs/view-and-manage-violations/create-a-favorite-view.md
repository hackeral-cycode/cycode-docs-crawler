## Overview [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

The Favorite Views feature enables you to to save personalized views on the violation screen, including filters, column selections, breadcrumb levels, and group-by aggregations. This feature improves your navigation efficiency, by minimizing efforts and clicks required to access preferred views.

This feature also facilitates collaboration by offering two options for sharing views:

*   **Copy URL sharing** (available for all users): Share a URL of your favorite view with other users, ensuring they have the necessary permissions to access the data or projects included in the view.
    
*   **Tenant-wide sharing** (admins only): Admins can share views with all tenant members, making the view available in every member’s view list.
    

Both sharing methods respect recipient permissions, ensuring privacy and security (note that users are limited to 30 favorite views). By providing these sharing options, the Favorite View feature enhances collaboration and simplifies navigation across the Cycode platform.

### Saving a favorite view [Copied!](#saving-a-favorite-view "Copy link to this section")

1.  To save a favorite view, configure the desired filters, column selections, breadcrumb levels, and group-by aggregations on the violation screen, then click the **star** button in the upper right corner toolbar.

![](/assets/e868374-image.png)

This opens the Save View dialog:

![](/assets/768b96f-image.png)

2.  Give the view a name and (optionally) select a color for the side menu (the default is gray).
    
3.  Click **Save**. The new view will appear in the left pane under the violation categories, replacing the **Get Started** indicator.
    
4.  To access your saved views, click the **Favorite Filters** section in the left navigation pane and select the desired view from the list.
    

![](/assets/image-631.png)

### Managing saved views [Copied!](#managing-saved-views "Copy link to this section")

1.  To rename, delete, or copy the link to a saved view, click the three-dot option menu next to the saved view.
    
2.  Select the desired action.
    

![](/assets/4198225-image.png) Remember to regularly review and manage saved views to stay within the limit of 30 favorite views per user.

### Sharing views [Copied!](#sharing-views "Copy link to this section")

When you share a view URL, you’ll need to verify that the recipient has appropriate permissions to access the shared data or projects.

#### Copy URL sharing method (all users) [Copied!](#copy-url-sharing-method-all-users "Copy link to this section")

1.  Click the three-dot option menu of the desired view and select Copy Link. ![](/assets/image-633.png)
    
2.  Share the copied URL with another user.
    
3.  Verify that the recipient has the appropriate permissions to access the data or projects in the view. If the recipient lacks the required permissions, they will not be able to see the shared data, maintaining privacy and security.
    

#### Tenant-wide sharing method (admins only) [Copied!](#tenant-wide-sharing-method-admins-only "Copy link to this section")

Admins can create and share views with all tenant members. Once shared:

*   The view will appear in all Cycode members’ view lists, under the Favorite Filters section.
    
*   All tenant members, regardless of their role, can use, edit for personal customization, or delete the view from their list.
    

Important notes

*   Views shared by admins will only be visible to members with permissions to access the projects included in the view. If a view contains projects outside a member’s permissions, the view will be disabled for that member.
*   This capability allows security experts to efficiently disseminate curated views to all Cycode members, streamlining knowledge sharing and alignment.
