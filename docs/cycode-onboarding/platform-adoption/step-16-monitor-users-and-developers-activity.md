# Step 16: Monitor users and developers activity

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/cycode-onboarding/platform-adoption/step-16-monitor-users-and-developers-activity.md "Edit this page")

Updated on May 26, 2024 • Published on May 23, 2024

* * *

The Developer Audit tab lets you track the activities of your developers in the Cycode platform to see which pull request (PR) action was taken, who are the users in the Cycode platform, the category and resource, when they last performed an action, and whether that action was reviewed. The Audit Logs let you review the actions taken by the members of your organization in the Cycode platform (see [Monitoring developer and user activity](/developer-experience/security-administrators/monitor-developer-activity) ).

You can also monitor any scans that originate from the CLI using the CLI logs, which include pre-commit and IDE scans (see [CLI Logs](/developer-experience/security-administrators/monitor-developer-activity/cli-logs) for details).
