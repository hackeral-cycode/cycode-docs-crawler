# Step 5: Integrate issue tracking and alerting systems

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/cycode-onboarding/getting-started-with-onboarding/step-5-integrate-issue-tracking-and-alerting-systems.md "Edit this page")

Updated on May 26, 2024 • Published on May 23, 2024

* * *

Next, integrate Cycode with your [issue tracking](/integrations/issue-tracking-ticketing-tools) and [alerting systems](/integrations/alerting-tools) . We’ve covered the most popular tracking and alerting systems currently in use, including Asana, Azure Boards, ClickUp, Jira, Jira Server, Jira Data Center, Service Now and Zendesk as issue tracking systems, and Microsoft Teams, Opsgenie, PagerDuty, Slack, Splunk, Sumo Logic and emails for alerts and notifications.

In addition to these tools, Cycode allows you to build your own webhooks to send notifications to any external service.

These tools can be used for opening tickets manually from the Violations page, sending notifications and opening tickets automatically with Cycode workflows, notifying when reports are generated, and more.
