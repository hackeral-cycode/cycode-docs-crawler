Cycode integrates to your source code management (SCM) system, build tools, artifacts repository and Cloud environment, to correlate your entire pipeline and provide insights about your environment.

That’s why the first step in your onboarding process is to begin integrating your technologies with Cycode - regardless of which Cycode module you purchased.

## Why integrate technologies? [Copied!](#why-integrate-technologies "Copy link to this section")

Cycode’s security scanners are an integral component of our Pipeline Security and Application Security Testing modules, and are designed to identify security violations based on your technology integrations, regardless which tools. Cycode’s Posture Management module connects all your external security tools for visualization, prioritization, and remediation through a single pane of glass, and is designed to correlate all your security violations from external tools to the technologies pipeline insights. Therefore, to ensure a complete, comprehensive onboarding experience, we highly recommend that you first integrate your technologies with Cycode.

Important

Cycode security scanners will initiate the security scanning process immediately after you integrate your technologies. Following the integration you should expect violations coming from Cycode scanners to appear in the Violations page.

Before you integrate your tools with Cycode, you’ll need to do the following:

1.  Verify you have an Admin or Owner role in Cycode’s platform.
2.  Determine which integrations you need to add to Cycode and verify you have the appropriate permissions to perform these integrations.
3.  Choose which specific organization, projects, or cloud assets you wish to include in the initial integration process. Cycode supports integrating all your tools, organizations, projects and assets. However, large organizations may choose to gradually integrate Cycode to their environment. Note that you can always add additional integrations as needed.
4.  If you do not wish Cycode security scanners to perform their scans immediately after the integration, or you wish only specific scanners to run, contact Cycode Support to disable the relevant scanners.
5.  Cycode can integrate the technologies that are managed in your premise through a broker - a service deployed in your premise that communicates with Cycode SaaS platform. If this type of integration is relevant for you, follow the [Cycode Broker](/cycode-settings/cycode-broker) documentation for details.
