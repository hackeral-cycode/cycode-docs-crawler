# Step 19: Stay informed about Cycode's latest developments

The Cycode platform is continuously improving, bringing you the latest in innovation. To discover the newest features, visit the “product updates” area within the platform by simply clicking the bell icon located at the top right corner of your screen.

Detailed information on all updates can be found in the [What’s New at Cycode](/what-s-new-in-the-cycode-platform/what-s-new-in-2024) section of the Cycode documentation portal. Additionally, if you want to ensure you’re always in the loop on the latest developments and announcements, sign up for our monthly email product updates [here](https://cycode.com/monthly-product-updates) .
