# Projects

Updated on Apr 24, 2025 • Published on Apr 24, 2025

* * *

Cycode can detect asset collisions across projects. When an asset is associated with more than one project, this may cause data duplication and rule collisions, and will affect the project metrics.

By default, the **Show asset collision warning** option is enabled. You can toggle it off or on in the Settings view under **Admin settings** > **Projects**.
