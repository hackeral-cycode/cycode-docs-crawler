## **Overview** [Copied!](#overview "Copy link to this section")

As part of Cycode’s continuous improvement approach, we’re happy to announce a new hotfix process for on-prem customers. The new process enables us to resolve specific critical service issues without requiring a full version update.

### **What is a critical issue?** [Copied!](#what-is-a-critical-issue "Copy link to this section")

A critical issue is an issue that has been submitted to Cycode for investigation, in accordance with our SLA, and Cycode agrees that it is a critical issue. This means the issue is a verifiable and reproducible failure of the software to operate in all material respects with the applicable software’s documentation, that severely impacts your use of the software. For example, if the software is down or not functioning and no workaround exists.

The resolution time of a critical issue depends on the complexity of the issue, and Cycode will provide continuous progress updates until the critical issue is resolved.

### **Why is this important?** [Copied!](#why-is-this-important "Copy link to this section")

Until now, fixes were provided as part of a full version update, which could result in longer-than-optimal wait time and potentially impact your security posture. Releasing a software patch as a hotfix allows us to handle critical issues with a quicker turnaround time.

### **What can I expect?** [Copied!](#what-can-i-expect "Copy link to this section")

#### **Main release** [Copied!](#main-release "Copy link to this section")

Full system updates will continue to be released quarterly to ensure the Cycode system remains up to date.

#### **Service-specific fixes** [Copied!](#service-specific-fixes "Copy link to this section")

For critical issues, we will provide an image of the specific service for you to apply. We will also provide clear instructions and support to assist with the update process.

## **Hotfix process** [Copied!](#hotfix-process "Copy link to this section")

Technical issues will be prioritized as follows:

*   **Critical issues** will be handled at the highest priority and resolved as quickly as reasonably possible.
*   **All other issues** will be resolved in the next scheduled release, in accordance with Cycode’s SLA.

Cycode will use the following procedure to identify and resolve a critical issue via a hotfix:

1.  **Identify the problem**: Issues reported by you or our internal teams will be thoroughly investigated to identify the source of the problem and develop a solution plan.
2.  **Categorize the severity**: Based on the information received from you, together with the results of our investigation, we will categorize the severity of the issue in place.
3.  **Create a targeted fix**: If the issue is categorized as critical, the fix will be developed as a software patch for the relevant service and won’t require a full version update.
4.  **Verify the hotfix**: The hotfix will be tested by us in the relevant environment to verify that it fixes the critical issue and doesn’t affect other services.
5.  **Deploy the hotfix**: Cycode support will deploy the hotfix in your on-prem environment.

## **Frequently Asked Questions** [Copied!](#frequently-asked-questions "Copy link to this section")

#### **What if I need a fix urgently, but my issue is not classified as critical?** [Copied!](#what-if-i-need-a-fix-urgently-but-my-issue-is-not-classified-as-critical "Copy link to this section")

 Issues that aren’t identified as critical will be addressed in the next release. If you feel that the wait time is too long, please discuss possible solutions with your Customer Success Manager.

#### How long does it take to test and release a hotfix? [Copied!](#how-long-does-it-take-to-test-and-release-a-hotfix "Copy link to this section")

The release timeline depends on the complexity of the issue and what testing is required. Some issues may require multiple rounds of testing or patches across multiple services.

#### **If I’m using an older version of Cycode, will I still receive support?** [Copied!](#if-im-using-an-older-version-of-cycode-will-i-still-receive-support "Copy link to this section")

Yes, as described in Cycode’s SLA, we will assist with answering questions and diagnosing issues. However, hotfixes are provided only for the last released version. If moving to the latest version can resolve your issues, we will recommend this step and provide any needed assistance.

#### **What should I do if I encounter a related issue after applying a hotfix?** [Copied!](#what-should-i-do-if-i-encounter-a-related-issue-after-applying-a-hotfix "Copy link to this section")

 Please contact our support team immediately. We will work with you to diagnose and resolve the problem.

Note

If you have any questions or concerns about the hot fix process, contact your customer success manager.
