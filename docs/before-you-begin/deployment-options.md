# Deployment options

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/before-you-begin/deployment-options.md "Edit this page")

Updated on Jan 25, 2026 • Published on Apr 24, 2025

* * *

Cycode offers several deployment options that can be used both for the PoC period as well as the ongoing operation of the platform:

*   **SaaS** Connect your organization’s integrated systems (SCMs, Cloud, CI/CD, workflows, etc.) with Cycode’s SaaS platform, which is operated on AWS through the **North Virginia** (us-east-1) and the **Frankfurt** (eu-central-1) data centers.
    
*   **Hybrid** [Cycode Broker](/cycode-settings/cycode-broker/) supports a hybrid integration between Cycode and your on-premise deployment, allowing Cycode to scan your code without gaining network access to your internal assets. This solution provides you with all the benefits of a SaaS deployment but still allows you to isolate your on-premise cluster from the world.
    
*   **On-Premise** [Cycode Enterprise Server](/cycode-enterprise-server/) supports using Cycode fully on premise where your data is stored on your own servers, and you install and manage the Cycode platform on your own hardware. This requires your company’s IT department to maintain the infrastructure on their own.
