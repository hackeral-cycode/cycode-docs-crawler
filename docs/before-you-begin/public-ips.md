# Public IP addresses

[](https://gitlab.com/cycodehq/documentation/cycode_docs/-/blob/main/src/content/before-you-begin/public-ips.md "Edit this page")

* * *

Cycode connects outbound to your self-managed or on-premises systems during scans and integration syncs. To allow this traffic through your firewall, add the following Cycode egress IP addresses to your allowlist.

## SCM integrations [Copied!](#scm-integrations "Copy link to this section")

These IPs are required for integrations with self-managed SCM systems (GitHub Enterprise Server, GitLab Self-Managed, Bitbucket Server, Azure DevOps Server, and Gerrit). Allow both inbound and outbound connectivity between your SCM instance and the IPs for your region.

### US SaaS [Copied!](#us-saas "Copy link to this section")

IP Address

`34.230.110.76`

`54.205.205.122`

`18.209.26.95`

`34.74.159.168`

`3.226.104.48`

### EU SaaS [Copied!](#eu-saas "Copy link to this section")

IP Address

`52.29.182.96`

`3.69.177.124`

Note

These IPs are also listed in each individual integration guide: [GitHub Enterprise Server](/integrations/scm-source-code-management-tools/github-enterprise-server/) , [GitLab Self-Managed](/integrations/scm-source-code-management-tools/gitlab-self-managed/) , [Bitbucket Server](/integrations/scm-source-code-management-tools/bitbucket-server/) , [Azure DevOps Server](/integrations/scm-source-code-management-tools/azure-devops-server/) , [Gerrit](/integrations/scm-source-code-management-tools/gerrit/) .
